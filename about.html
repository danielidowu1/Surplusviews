Our Story

Who We Are

Surplusviews Global Limited is a professional consulting firm based in Lagos, Nigeria. We specialise in research support, career advisory, corporate intelligence, policy development, and training — bringing together a team of experienced researchers, analysts, and advisors committed to delivering exceptional results.

We were founded on a simple but powerful observation: too many capable students, professionals, and organisations lack access to the quality research and advisory support they need to succeed. We set out to change that — making world-class support simple, easy, and smart.

Today, we work with students, universities, NGOs, government agencies, and private sector organisations across Nigeria and beyond, delivering tailored solutions that drive real outcomes.
